About Me

Hey, I'm Nadia.

I spent 10 years as a backend developer at DataArt and the International Atomic Energy Agency before moving into AI automation consulting.

Now I work with founders and teams to bring practical AI automation into their businesses — from identifying the right opportunities to designing workflows in n8n or Claude and seeing the whole thing through to a working system.

I also teach professionals who want to actually understand AI, whether they're looking to introduce automation at their company or just future-proof their career.

Everything I teach, I use. My focus is hands-on automation for operations, marketing, and content — the same systems running in my own business right now.

I work best with people who've decided AI is part of their future and are ready to put in the work.
If you're looking for magic buttons, I'm not your person.
